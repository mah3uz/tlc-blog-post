A specialty within medicine that deals with conditions of the ear, nose, and throat (ENT) and related structures of the head and neck. Doctors who specialize in this area are called ENT doctors, ENT surgeons, head and neck surgeons or otolaryngologists. Procedures performed by such specialists may include a grommet or tonsillectomy.

Ear problems include the examination and management of tumours, hearing loss, balance problems and cosmetics disorders. Nasal conditions may include management of nasal obstruction, allergies, sinusitis and cosmetic nasal deformities. Head and neck surgery include the management of thyroid and parotid disease as well as upper digestive tract tumours.
